# Invited Tester Round — v9.4

## Group

Five trusted people, preferably at least three Android users, using different property photographs.

## Instruction to tester

“Open VerdeAI, use one property photo, and tell me what you think it is offering. I will not explain it first.”

## Five-minute sequence

1. Upload one real property photo.
2. Choose the closest starter clue and create the board.
3. Explain in their own words what `Your Property Today`, the six futures and the recommendation mean.
4. Open at least three futures.
5. Select a future different from the recommendation.
6. Open it full size and try Adjust placement / Done placing concept.
7. Refresh and confirm the selection and placement remain.
8. Give one sentence: useful, confusing or not believable, and why.

## Record

- Phone model and browser.
- Property type.
- Recommendation.
- Selected future.
- Whether the six futures felt visibly different.
- Whether the original property remained clear.
- Whether Recommended versus Selected was understood.
- Whether the first move was clear.
- Whether the five-year strip helped or confused.
- One board screenshot.
- One unedited tester sentence.

## Stop conditions

Pause the round for broken controls, lost persistence, mismatched future text/image, linked Recommended and Selected states, blank output, crashes, or any unexpected backend/payment/rendering state.

Cosmetic plant-edge, crowding and curved-line complaints should be recorded and grouped. They do not automatically stop the round unless they prevent understanding.
